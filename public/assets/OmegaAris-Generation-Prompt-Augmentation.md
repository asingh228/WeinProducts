## Product Identity

The product is the **OMEGA ARIS-660B Bullion Coin Tester** by **Wein Products Inc.**

It is a glossy black desktop bullion coin testing device. It combines:

- A low-profile black base.
- A black foam testing pad.
- A gold coin under a haptic resonator/test probe.
- A vertically mounted smartphone.
- A small cable-connected tapping/clicker device on the phone screen.
- A black-and-gold OMEGA ARIS-660B branding decal across the rear/top of the base.

The product should feel like a premium technical instrument, not a generic phone stand.

## Overall Device Shape

The device has a **wide, slim, shallow, curved wedge-shaped base**.

This is one of the most important consistency points.

The base must be:

- Low-profile.
- Wide and stable.
- Glossy black.
- Rounded at the front edge.
- Slightly curved/arched across the top surface.
- Slim when viewed from the left or right side.
- Supported by small rounded feet.

The base must **not** become:

- Thick.
- Fat.
- Boxy.
- Tall.
- Rectangular like a block.
- A generic electronics enclosure.

For 45-degree left and 45-degree right views, the side profile should clearly show a shallow, elegant curved wedge. The base should look wide and low, not chunky.

## Critical Cable and Screen Clicker Detail

This is a required product detail.

There is one thin black cable that comes from the rear/left/back area and loops forward to a small black tapping/clicker device mounted against the lower-left area of the phone screen.

The clicker device should be located near the lower-left quadrant of the phone display, around the rating/star area. It should touch the phone screen, not float in space.

The clicker should look like:

- A small matte-black cylindrical puck or short barrel.
- Black-on-black with subtle highlights.
- Compact.
- Attached to or resting against the screen.
- Connected by a thin black cable entering from the left or rear.
- Placed on the phone screen itself, slightly in from the left edge, around the `3 STARS` / star-rating area.
- Oriented flat against the screen, with the circular face parallel to the phone glass.

The clicker should **not** be placed at the extreme bottom-left edge of the phone. It should not hang off the bezel, float beside the phone, or sit too high near the `RESULT: PASS` text. It should be near the rating area, similar to the approved front shot.

The clicker must **not** have:

- A gray top.
- A silver top.
- A shiny metallic top.
- A chrome cap.
- A bright reflective face.
- A protruding nub, tab, lip, or extension that sticks out beyond the circular diameter of the clicker face.
- A weird offset lower piece where it touches the screen.

The clicker’s face/top should remain black or very dark charcoal. Any highlight should be subtle and caused by lighting, not a different material color. The contact face should read as one clean, simple circular black piece.

Do not omit the cable/clicker assembly unless explicitly requested.

Use professional studio product photography lighting.

Recommended style:

- Seamless white or very light gray background.
- Softbox reflections.
- Controlled glossy highlights.
- Sharp product focus.
- Mild contact shadows under the base.
- Premium black-and-gold contrast.
- Realistic metallic gold on the coin and decal.

The glossy black base should have realistic reflections but should not look wet, greasy, oily, or overly mirror-like.

The foam pad should remain matte and textured. Do not make the foam glossy.

The phone screen should be legible and free of flash glare.

Avoid amateur household context: no carpet, furniture, flash reflections, fingerprints, random room lighting, or background clutter.
